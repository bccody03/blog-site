About

I'm Blake Cody — 22 years old, living in Michigan. One day I want to be an author and a coach for life and relationships: someone who helps people work through what they're carrying and build a life that actually feels like their own.

For now, I write on Substack — honest thoughts and lessons pulled straight from my own life, shared in the hope that they might help someone out. Alongside it I'm writing my book, Aligned: Lead with Love, Live with Intention, figuring all of this out in real time and writing down what's working and what isn't as I go.

This site mirrors my Substack — subscribe there to get every piece in your inbox.
