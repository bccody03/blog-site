The book

Aligned

Lead with Love, Live with Intention

I'm in the process of becoming an author — and honestly, it still feels surreal to write that. Publishing a book has recently become a dream of mine. Not for my name on a cover, but because I want to put something into the world that genuinely helps people with their relationships and the way they live their lives.

Aligned is that book. It's a field guide for anyone rebuilding after heartbreak and trying to become someone they're proud of — three parts: decoding love, building yourself, and living with intention. Written straight from inside the work. No guru act. No fluff.

The first chapter is available to read right here, for free. If it reaches just one person — if it helps one of you become more confident in your own life and step into becoming a new person — then every word of it was worth writing.
